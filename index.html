Making An Informed Decision

Making An Informed Decision (MAID) is a flagship event organised annually by MMI Young Medics. MAID 2022 will be the 6th consecutive MAID event since 2017. The name ‘Making An Informed Decision’ speaks for itself as it perfectly conveys the purpose of the event as a whole. Pre-university is the period in our lives when we would need to make crucial decisions regarding our future careers. For some people, it is a dream come true to finally be able to pursue their passions. However, some will inevitably find themselves in a dilemma, second-guessing their capabilities and interests. Through MAID 2022, we aim to support and assist pre-medical students in their decision-making processes on whether pursuing a medical degree is suitable for them.

Our Activities

We hope to provide an opportunity for you to gain some exposure to medicine through various virtual taster activities such as ‘Keynote Speeches’, ‘Doctors in the House’ and ‘Simulated Emergency: T.R.I.A.G.E.’

Keynote Speakers

We hope to provide participants a bird's eye view of the journey as a doctor with these 2 topics:
1. Medicine: Is It All About Expectations?
2. Medicine: Clinical V.S. Non-Clinical Field

Doctor In The House

Doctors in the House is a medical history taking session that features participants role-playing as doctors and as patients. In this activity, participants will be given the opportunity to learn how to perform the medical interview by taking history from simulated patients.

T.R.I.A.G.E.

To Respond Immediately And Gauge Effectively (or T.R.I.A.G.E for short) is an exciting problem-based game packed with action and fun that is sure to leave you on the edge of your seat once you’ve accomplished all tasks, each more daunting than the last!
